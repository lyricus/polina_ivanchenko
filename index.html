About the Project

You are not alone in this city

Cultural Bridge Ambassadors is a student-led initiative connecting Ukrainian newcomers with local Slovenian students in Ljubljana. We believe that the best way to feel at home is through shared experiences, honest information, and genuine friendship.

This guide was created by young people who have gone through the same questions you are asking right now: Where do I get a SIM card? Which coffee shop has the fastest Wi-Fi? How do I use the Urbana bus card? We have answered all of these — and more.

Whether you arrived last week or last year, this is your starting point. Explore the city, reach out, and let us help you build your Ljubljana story.

Quick Tips

First things first

The four things to sort out in your very first week.

📱

Get a SIM card

Telekom Slovenia or A1 — shops in the city center. Bring your passport.

🚌

Buy an Urbana card

The green rechargeable card for all buses. Get it at Urbanija kiosks or LPP website.

🏥

Register at a doctor

Choose a personal physician (osebni zdravnik) within the first month of arrival.

📋

Report your address

Register your temporary residence at the Administrative Unit (Upravna enota).
